[h1]Storage Tooltip[/h1]

See what's inside your storage bins without clicking on them!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Shows storage contents when hovering over any storage building
[*] Groups identical items (e.g., "Iron Ore x5 (127.3 kg)")
[*] Displays total mass vs capacity
[*] Works with all storage buildings (Storage Bin, Smart Storage Bin, Refrigerator, etc.)
[*] Seamlessly integrated - looks like a vanilla feature!
[/list]

[h2]How to Use[/h2]
Simply hover your mouse over any storage building and you'll see a "STORAGE CONTENTS" section in the tooltip showing:
[list]
[*] Item names with quantities
[*] Mass per item type
[*] Total stored mass vs capacity
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*] Works with vanilla ONI and Spaced Out DLC
[*] No known mod conflicts
[*] Safe to add to existing saves
[/list]

[h2]Technical Details[/h2]
This mod uses Harmony to patch the hover tooltip system, adding storage information without replacing or interfering with vanilla tooltips.
